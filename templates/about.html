About Us

No meal plan, no worries! Here at LINKtohealth, we are dedicated to helping people like you serve up delicous, healthy meals in the comfort of your own home and without the expense of having purchase new ingredients. Simply let us know what food items you have in your fridge to work with and we'll provide you savory recipes that you can make in no time! Founded by four food-loving low-budget college students, we know how stressful cooking for oneself can be without much to spend or experinece in whipping up creative, healthy food options. Trust us to deliver you delightful, exquisite meals and you'll never have to stress about making a meal ever again!
